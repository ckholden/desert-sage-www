Service Areas

Boutique Vacation Rental Management Across Oregon

Rooted in Central Oregon, Desert Sage Rentals is proud to serve homeowners across the many corners of Oregon -- especially the ones that feel a little quieter, a little more special. We love the properties tucked away on rural roads, perched in small towns, or set in the "hidden gem" destinations that make guests feel like they've discovered something unforgettable.

From cozy mountain cabins and high-desert homesteads to riverside hideouts, wine-country cottages, and coastal escapes, we welcome unique homes of all kinds.

Our owner-led, hands-on style keeps things personal, and our focus on smaller communities allows us to bring genuine hospitality wherever we go.

Not sure if your property is in our service area? Just ask -- chances are, we're already familiar with your part of Oregon, and we'd love to help.
