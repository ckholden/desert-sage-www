Boutique management, rooted in Central Oregon

Desert Sage Rentals looks after a small, hand-picked collection of homes across Central Oregon and the Columbia River Gorge — from a reservoir-view retreat to walkable downtown cottages. We're locally owned and run, and we keep it that way on purpose: staying small lets us give every home the attention a big management company can't.

We run the whole operation so owners don't have to — listing and dynamic pricing, guest communication, professional cleaning and turnovers, smart-lock self check-in, maintenance, and round-the-clock support. Your property gets cared for like it's our own.
