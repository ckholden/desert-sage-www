What We Do

Desert Sage Rentals is your boutique vacation rental partner, rooted in Central Oregon and thoughtfully expanding throughout Oregon's most memorable places. We're drawn to the quieter corners of the state -- the rural roads, small towns, mountain communities, river bends, and coastal hideaways that give Oregon its charm. These are the homes and destinations where hospitality still feels personal, and we're proud to help them shine.

We offer full-service vacation rental management with a hands-on, owner-led approach. From reservations and cleaning coordination to guest communication and 24/7 support, we handle every detail with care. Staying intentionally small allows us to give each property the tailored attention big companies simply can't offer.

For homeowners launching their first rental, our consulting services make the process clear, grounded, and stress-free -- whether your property is in a well-loved destination or tucked away in a hidden gem across the state.

At Desert Sage Rentals, every home matters and every guest experience counts. We bring neighborly hospitality to every stay, no matter where your property lives on the map.
